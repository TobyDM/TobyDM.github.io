Emotional Cubes

Conveying Six Emotions and T-Shirt Design

Challenged to convey Six Different Emotions (Happy, Surprised, Embarrased, Sad, Angry, and Tired), I created a universe of cube headed characters with simplistic, blob-like bodies. This idea was then expanded for 4 of the emotions as T-Shirt Designs for "Diarchipretti".
