The Sleeps

T-Shirts and Sticker Design.

A band is a business. In addition to the music, a band needs to get a buzz going, and it needs to raise money. Band merchandise helps in this effort, and I am the creator of clothing and stickers for The Sleeps. With merchandise, I have helped create a brand for our band. Using a similar style across t-shirts and stickers, people can see our name in the world in various pieces of merchandise and recognize "The Sleeps."

The Sleeps are a big wave band from Oakland, California who play shows around the Bay Area and Santa Cruz. I'm the drummer.
