The Sleeps

T-Shirts and Sticker Design.

The Sleeps is a big wave band from Oakland California. We are about two years old now, and are constantly playing shows around the Bay Area and Santa Cruz. In order to continue growing as a band, we need to get our name out and raise money. With merchandise, I have helped create a brand for our band. With a similar style across t-shirts and stickers, people can see our name in the world from multiple angles and recognize "The Sleeps."
